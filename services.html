Our Services

At Keljoe, we offer a variety of services to help you with your classic car needs. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or restore a classic car, we've got you covered.

Services We Offer

Classic Car Sales

Looking to buy a classic car? We have a wide selection of classic cars for sale. Whether you're looking for a project car or a fully restored classic, we can help you find the perfect car for your needs.

Classic Car Restoration

Have a classic car that needs some TLC? Our team of expert mechanics and restoration specialists can help you restore your classic car to its former glory. From minor repairs to full restorations, we can handle any job, big or small.

Classic Car Appraisals

Need to know the value of your classic car? We offer classic car appraisals to help you determine the value of your classic car. Whether you're looking to sell your car or just want to know how much it's worth, we can help.

Classic Car Consignment

Looking to sell your classic car? We offer classic car consignment services to help you sell your car quickly and easily. We'll handle all the details, from advertising to paperwork, so you can sit back and relax while we find a buyer for your car.

Classic Car Rentals

Want to experience the thrill of driving a classic car? We offer classic car rentals so you can take a trip down memory lane in style. Whether you're looking for a weekend getaway or a special occasion, we have a classic car for you.
